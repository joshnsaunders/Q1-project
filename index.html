Find the best public schools.
DPS assigns blue to distinguished schools with the highest achieving students. The seats at these schools fill up quickly. Less than 10% of DPS schools are blue. We recommend applying.
DPS assigns green to schools that meet expectations. About one third of schools in DPS are green. We'd apply for a seat at these schools too.
DPS assigns yellow to schools that are "Accredited on Watch" and are expected to raise their student achievement levels among other requirements. About 25% of schools in DPS are yellow.
DPS assigns orange to schools that are "Accredited on Priority Watch" and meet less than 40% of the expectations set by Denver Public Schools. About 5% of schools are orange.
DPS assigns red to schools that are "Accredited on Probation" and meet less than 33% of the expectations set by Denver Public Schools. About 12% of schools are red.
Not all public schools are created the same. We're here to make sure you find the right one for you and your kids.
